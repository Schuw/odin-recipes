The best brazilian Pastel recipe

Crispy fried pastry filled with savory or sweet ingredients, popular in Brazilian street food culture a thin, rectangular dough folded over fillings like cheese, meat, or banana with cinnamon, then deep-fried until golden.

ingredients

Everything you will need to make this dish

🥟 Dough ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon Cachaça (Brazilian sugarcane liquor – helps make the dough crispier)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/2 cup warm water (approximately)

🧀 Filling ingredients (simple cheese pastel):

  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded or sliced (as needed)
  • Oregano (optional)

🛢️ For frying:

  • vegetable oil (enough for deep frying)

Steps to make it delicious:

1. Make the Dough

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour and salt.
  2. Add the oil and cachaça, and stir.
  3. Slowly add warm water, mixing until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5–7 minutes until smooth.
  5. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.

2. Prepare the Filling

  1. While the dough rests, shred or slice the mozzarella cheese.
  2. a pinch of oregano if desired.

3. Roll and fill the dough

  1. Divide the dough into small portions
  2. Roll each portion into a thin rectangle (the thinner, the crispier).
  3. Place cheese on one half of the dough.
  4. Fold it over and press the edges with a fork to seal.

4. Fry the Beauty

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan over medium-high heat.
  2. When the oil is hot (about 350°F / 180°C), fry each pastel until golden and crispy (about 2–3 minutes per side).
  3. Remove and drain on paper towels.

5. You did it! Just enjoy your amazing dish!
